Data hubs

National Road Safety Data Hub

Provides national road safety dashboards, crash data, performance tracking, and interactive tools that supplement BIACTRE's Road Safety statistics.

Visit datahub.roadsafety.gov.au

National Freight Data Hub (NFDH)

Aggregates and visualises Australia's freight data across road, rail, air, and maritime. Includes freight movement datasets, mapping tools, and dashboards.

Visit freightdata.gov.au

Regional Data Hub

Offers widely used datasets and tools focused on regional Australia — population, housing, economy, social outcomes, and more. Supports regional planning and analysis.

Visit regionaldata.gov.au
